ANALYTICS PIPELINE

  • SOLVING CONCEPT DRIFT

    TS2Vec + MOMEMTO. Traditional forecasting fails as machines wear out and patterns change ("Concept Drift"). We use TS2Vec with a Memory Gate (MOMEMTO) to stabilize learning, ensuring the model doesn't over-generalize or "forget" the baseline behavior over weeks of operation.

  • CHANGE POINT DETECTION

    Bayesian CPD. We use Bayesian algorithms to identify structural shifts in consumption—distinguishing between a normal cycle change and a motor seizing up.

  • COMPLIANCE ENGINE

    EU Digital Product Passport. Energy logs are cryptographically signed and saved in Parquet format. This allows you to generate a "Green Certificate" per batch, satisfying strict EU CBAM audits.

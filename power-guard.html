High-frequency energy analytics and forecasting.
EU CBAM & DPP Compliant.

THE ENERGY CRISIS

Factories in Gazipur operate at 30% capacity due to LNG shortages. Captive diesel generation costs $0.32/kWh. Using the PZEM-004T split-core sensor, we monitor power at 1Hz.

Peak Shaving: This enables automated shedding of non-critical loads 180 seconds *before* a spike trips the generator, saving roughly $180/month per line.

ANALYTICS PIPELINE

  • VECTORIZATION

    TS2Vec + MOMEMTO. Traditional forecasting fails due to "concept drift". Our memory gate patch allows the model to retain a baseline over months of operation.

  • CHANGE POINT

    Bayesian CPD. Identifies structural changes in consumption patterns (e.g., a motor seizing up) to trigger preventative maintenance.

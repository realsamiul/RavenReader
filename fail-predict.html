PHYSICS OF FAILURE

Mechanical faults (like a pitted bearing or misaligned shaft) emit specific micro-harmonics long before they generate audible noise or heat. By attaching a $1.60 MEMS sensor (MPU-6050) to the motor housing, we capture 3-axis acceleration data. [attachment_0](attachment)

Using Fast Fourier Transform (FFT), we decompose this vibration data into frequency spectrums. A spike at a specific frequency (e.g., 500Hz) acts as a fingerprint for specific failure modes like bearing race degradation, allowing us to predict failure 5 hours before it occurs.
